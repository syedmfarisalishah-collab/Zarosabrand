Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025 · Zarosa Brand, Pakistan

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Zarosa Brand collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you shop with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you shop at Zarosa Brand, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Delivery address and city
  • Order history and product preferences
  • Payment method (we do not store card numbers)
  • Device information and browsing data for website improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process and deliver your orders, send order confirmation and tracking updates via SMS/WhatsApp, provide customer support, and improve our products and services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Payment Security

For digital payments (EasyPaisa, JazzCash, Debit/Credit Card), transactions are processed securely. We do not store payment credentials on our servers. Cash on Delivery requires no digital payment data.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to remember your cart and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We share your delivery address and contact details with our delivery partners (Leopards, PostEx, EasyShip, Tranzo, Dex) solely for the purpose of delivering your order. No other sharing occurs without your consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us via Instagram @zarosabrandofficial for any privacy-related requests.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via Instagram: @zarosabrandofficial

Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025 · 7-Day Return Window

✅ Zarosa Brand offers a hassle-free 7-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we'll make it right.

Return Window

You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order. After 7 days, we are unable to accept returns unless the product is defective.

Eligible Return Conditions

  • Item is unused, unworn, and unwashed
  • Original tags and packaging intact
  • Item is in the same condition as received
  • Return request submitted within 7 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

  • Beauty and skincare products (opened)
  • Undergarments and swimwear
  • Items on final sale / clearance
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse

How to Return

  • Message us on Instagram: @zarosabrandofficial
  • Share your order ID and reason for return
  • We will arrange pickup or guide you to drop off the parcel
  • Refund or exchange processed within 3–5 business days of receiving the return

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to your original payment method. For COD orders, refunds are issued via EasyPaisa or JazzCash bank transfer within 3–5 business days.

Exchange Policy

We offer size/color exchanges subject to stock availability. Contact us via Instagram to check availability before initiating a return.

Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2025 · Free Delivery Nationwide

🚚 All orders at Zarosa Brand ship FREE across Pakistan — no minimum order required.

Delivery Partners

We work with Pakistan's most reliable couriers to ensure safe, timely delivery:

  • 🐆 Leopards Courier — Nationwide coverage
  • 📦 PostEx — COD specialist, fast delivery
  • ✈️ EasyShip — Express options
  • 🚛 Tranzo — Bulk & heavy orders
  • 📬 Dex — Last-mile delivery

Delivery Timeframes

  • Major Cities (Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi): 2–3 working days
  • Other Cities (Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, etc.): 3–5 working days
  • Remote Areas: 5–7 working days

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after confirmation. You will receive an SMS or WhatsApp message with your tracking number once dispatched. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are processed the next business day.

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, use our Order Tracking page → or visit the courier's website with your tracking number to follow your parcel in real time.

Failed Deliveries

If the courier is unable to deliver (wrong address, recipient unavailable), they will attempt delivery up to 2 more times. After that, the parcel is returned to us and a re-delivery fee may apply.

Damaged Orders

If your order arrives damaged, photograph the package and item immediately and contact us via Instagram @zarosabrandofficial. We will arrange a replacement or refund.
